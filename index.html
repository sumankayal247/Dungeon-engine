WELCOME TO DUNGEON ENGINE 🏰

A top-down dungeon shooter you can design yourself.

To play: move with WASD, aim & shoot with the arrow keys (or the two on-screen sticks on mobile). Grab weapons & powerups, then fight to the glowing exit portal. Esc pauses.

To build: hit ✎ Edit (top-right) to draw your own dungeon, or paste a JSON level in the left panel. No JSON? Hit ⧉ Copy Level Prompt, give it to any AI, and paste back what it returns.

To share: pause → Share / Copy Link packs your whole dungeon into a URL.

You can reopen this anytime with the ? Help button.
